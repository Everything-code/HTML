Food Groups:
Protein

A Protein is one or more amino acid.

  1. Complete
  2. In-Complete
Carbohydrate/Fiber

A Carbohydrate is an organic molecule with carbon, hydrogen and oxygen.

A Fiber is a carbohydrate from plant source.
